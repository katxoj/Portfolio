About Me

Kate James

Hello! I'm Kate James, a passionate front-end web developer and an avid Paramore fan that incorporates my love for music into technology. My love for Paramore started on my somber bus rides home from Middle School. It's grown into being one of my biggest inspirations of all time. Exploring my favorite music fuels my creativity, whether I'm designing user-friendly websites or learning songs on my guitar.

As a Computer Science student at Tech University, I specialize in creating responsive and engaging digital experiences. From crafting sleek portfolio websites to developing interactive web applications, I enjoy merging technology with creativity. My skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React allow me to build intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me admiring my CD collection or rewatching my favorite movies over and over again, or endlessly scrolling on Twitter.

My goal is to inspire others to embrace both technology and their interests. Whether you're looking for web development insights or reccommendations on my favorite Paramore tracks, I'd love to connect.
