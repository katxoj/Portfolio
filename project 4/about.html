About Me

Jane Doe

Hello! I'm Jane Doe, a passionate front-end web developer and an avid hiker who finds inspiration both in code and on the trails. My love for nature started with a simple weekend hike, but it quickly evolved into a lifelong passion that led me to form my own hiking group. Exploring the great outdoors fuels my creativity, whether I’m designing user-friendly websites or mapping out the next adventure.

As a Computer Science student at Tech University, I specialize in creating responsive and engaging digital experiences. From crafting sleek portfolio websites to developing interactive web applications, I enjoy merging technology with creativity. My skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React allow me to build intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me leading my hiking group on breathtaking trails across the country. Whether it’s conquering rugged mountain peaks, trekking through dense forests, or simply enjoying a peaceful lakeside walk, I believe that every hike is an opportunity to disconnect from the digital world and reconnect with nature.

My goal is to inspire others to embrace both technology and the outdoors. Whether you're looking for web development insights or recommendations on scenic hiking trails, I’d love to connect! Let's create, explore, and make every step—both online and on the trail—an adventure worth taking.
